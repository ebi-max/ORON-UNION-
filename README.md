# ORON UNION

**ORON UNION** is a community-driven mobile app dedicated to uniting the people of Oron, Akwa Ibom State. The app aims to promote our social, cultural, and traditional activities, connecting Oron people far and near.

---

## Mission
To foster unity, preserve our rich cultural heritage, and promote social development among the Oron people through a vibrant digital platform.

---

## Features
- Connect with fellow Oron people worldwide
- Promote social and cultural activities
- Stay updated with local news and events
- Share photos and videos of cultural festivals and celebrations
- Participate in discussion forums
- Access local marketplace and services
- Find community leaders and organizations
